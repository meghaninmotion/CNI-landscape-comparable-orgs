# CNI-landscape-comparable-orgs
A prototype exploration of the mission and values landscape of comparable organizations to CNI

This multi-tab exploration represents the mission and values of 19 organizations that are comparable in purpose, practice, and membership to CNI. This resource maps the dominant themes and commitments across 19 library and IT member organization mission and vision statements, indicating overlaps and differentiators across three tabs: a themes + organizations matrix and themes overview. 

While developing this presentation using Claude,ai, the generative AI suggested a differentiators tab. This view has been included for the summary insights it offers; however, generative AI chatbots are known to hallucinate or craft inaccurate or fabricated details. Please provide feedback to improve the organizational summaries in that tab. Data were gathered from these organizations' websites and the call outs these sites make about mission, vision, and values.

This exploraton was developed by pairing with Claude AI to develop html to represent the overlapping and distinct objectives of similar organizations to CNI. It is presented for exploration. It can be further developed with your feedback and suggestions. Please share those thoughts - and report errors - as pull requests!
